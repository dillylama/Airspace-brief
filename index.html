How this works

It reads your saved ATNS RSA-DATA file and your AOI, then tells you the frequency you'd use and what's around you — filtered to the heights you actually operate at, not the airways far overhead.
  1. Load your ATNS KMZ (kept locally, so the site being down doesn't matter).
  2. Give it an AOI: load a KML/KMZ, or just drop a point on the map / type coordinates.
  3. Set your max height of interest, then generate the brief.
Nothing is hardcoded — every frequency, limit and area comes out of your own file, with the raw text shown so you can check it.
